SMMU news archive

These are the news pages from my website (my blog, essentially) while I was writing SMMU. Many of the links may be broken. Some of these are a bit juvenile in places - I was only 17!
  • 1999-01-14 - 1999-02-19:
    My first initial discussions about writing a Doom source port, including screenshots of "sfdoom" (which was based on the Chi Hoang sources). Before long I abandon this and restart my work based on MBF as well. I appeal to the public for a name for the port and settle on SMMU. The year on this page is listed as 1998 but I think this is a mistake.
  • 1999-03-20 - 1999-09-18:
    First mentions of FraggleScript, and I make several SMMU releases. I unintentionally start an argument with ZDoom author Randy Heit.
  • 1999-09-18 - 2000-04-17:
    Linux port, hubs and netcode development. First release with FraggleScript incorporated.
